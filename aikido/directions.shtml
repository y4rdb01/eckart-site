Dojo Directions

Via I-85:
  • Take US-280 West (towards Opelika), exit 58
  • Travel 0.4 miles
  • Turn RIGHT onto Gateway Drive/US-280
  • Travel 1.5 miles
  • Turn LEFT onto Pepperell Parkway/AL-14/AL-15/AL-38
  • Travel 4.5 miles
  • Turn LEFT onto North Gay Street
  • Take 1st RIGHT onto Mitcham Avenue
  • Take 1st LEFT onto North College Street
  • Take 2nd RIGHT onto West Glenn Avenue
  • Take 1st RIGHT onto North Donahue Drive
  • Tkae 2nd LEFT onto Martin Luther King Drive
  • Take 2nd RIGHT onto Boykin Street
  • Boykin Community Center is on the right

From Columbus:
  • From I-185, take exit 10 onto US-80 WEST
  • Travel 6 miles
  • Bear RIGHT onto US-431/US-280 North
  • Travel 22.1 miles
  • Take the I-85 south bound exit, see "Via I-85" instructions above.
