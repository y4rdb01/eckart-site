Riddle Answer

find   .   \(   \!   -type   d   -perm   -005   -prune   \)   -o   \(   -type   f   -perm   -004   -print   \)

Prints the files in the current directory structure that are readable by others, making sure to skip over any files that are NOT readable and any directories that are NEITHER readable nor executable. Notice that parentheses and bang must be escaped by the use of backslashes in order not to be interpreted by the shell before the arguments are passed to find.
