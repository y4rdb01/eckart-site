Dr. J Dana Eckart: Submitting Assignments

  1. Login to CougarView
  2. Select the course you're submitting the assignment for
  3. Click on the "Dropbox" button (4th from the left) in the big blue bar at the top of the page.
  4. Click on the appropriate Assignment # folder.
  5. If your submission is composed of multiple files, please zip them up (remember to specify recursive traversal of directories if appropriate). If you don't already have zip, gzip is free and should do the trick.
  6. Click the "Add a File" button.
  7. No comment is necessary
  8. Click submit and then go have a nice hot cup of Indian Chai Tea (if you're so inclined).
