An Impossible Pinball Game

Agents (balls) have a direction of travel (north, south, east, or west) and continue traveling in that direction until they encounter either a bumper (bumper field equal 1) or another ball. When a bumper is encountered the ball splits into two, each new ball traveling in one of the orthogonal directions. If two balls collide, then they annihilate one another. The display field is used to provide a composite picture of balls and bumpers.
