"...Then we hit on the idea of a team building theme song. Kind of like the the school fight song. By strange coincidence, we all like Barry Manilow and really thought his best work was 'I write the songs..' So with that in mind we penned our own lyrics that we could sing along to this melody. It came out so good and catchy that I thought I better share it here with all of you!

The contributions were from testers, developers and techpubbers.
Enjoy it!!

[authors unknown]
